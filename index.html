Our hedgehogs are 100% crafted by our local artisans. Each quill is perfectly put into place and we make sure that each hedgehog has at least 4000 quills. The hedgehogs we produce go through a rigorous inspection and must pass this test before being shipped out to you. Part of this testing program includes checking the buoyancy of the hoglet. All hoglets are fed 100% organic vegetables sourced locally by our professionally trained farmers. They also are fed organic farm fresh insects. Each hedgehog purchase includes a starter package, that includes: One (1) specialized feed bag, an owner instruction manual, and one (1) free random sweater and hat combination set for your prickle pal, when you sign up with our newsletter!

Shipping

Enjoy high speed shipping across the globe! We pride ourselves in how fast our products go out. Since the nature of our business are live animals, we understand the importance of getting out little guys out to their new parents quickly.
